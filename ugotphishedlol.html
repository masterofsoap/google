You Were Almost Phished!

You have been redirected to this page because you attempted to log in to a phishing demonstration site. Phishing is a type of online scam where attackers impersonate legitimate websites to steal your personal information.

Here are some tips to protect yourself from phishing attacks:

  • Always check the URL of the website before entering your login information.
  • Look for HTTPS and a padlock icon in the address bar.
  • Be cautious of unsolicited emails or messages asking for personal information.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for an extra layer of security.
